Please Donate!

Your donation will go to the flood affected people. This donation matters too much to us and the people in need. They will be back again to their normal life hopefully with the help of your donation.

Our Donation Plans

Food for a family

Education of a child includes necessary books because their books khata and other stuff are spoiled because of flood. They need their study stuffs very soon.
